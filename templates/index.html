About Keylogger Threats

Keyloggers are malicious programs that record keystrokes to steal sensitive information like:

  • Passwords and login credentials
  • Credit card numbers and banking information
  • Personal messages and documents
  • Social security numbers and other sensitive data

Our detection tool uses advanced behavioral analysis to identify these threats before they can compromise your data.
